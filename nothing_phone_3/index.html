Nothing Phone 3

A Flagship with Unique Design and LED Glyph Experience

Overview

The Nothing Phone 3, launched in July 2025, is a unique mid-to-flagship device designed for users seeking a stylish, minimalist smartphone with cutting-edge features. With its transparent design, AMOLED display, and LED Glyph system, it stands out in the crowded smartphone market, combining solid performance, long-lasting battery, and innovative notifications in a visually striking form factor.

Design and Build

The Phone 3 features a transparent rear panel with an aluminum frame and Gorilla Glass front and back. It has a premium feel and is lightweight at ~218 grams. IP68 certification ensures water and dust resistance. Its minimalist and futuristic look makes it instantly recognizable and highly appealing for users who prioritize style.

Main Display

The main 6.67-inch AMOLED display delivers Full HD+ resolution and a fixed 120Hz refresh rate, providing bright, vivid colors and smooth scrolling. It is ideal for gaming, multimedia, and daily use, though lacks adaptive refresh rate, which may affect battery efficiency slightly under heavy use.

Special Features

One of the signature innovations of Nothing Phone 3 is its LED Glyph system and the small rear display. The rear display shows notifications, music playback, incoming calls, and visual effects through the LED Glyph, allowing users to get key information without opening the phone. This feature enhances the user experience and adds a unique visual identity to the device.

Performance and Hardware

Powered by the Snapdragon 8s Gen 4 and paired with 12GB or 16GB RAM, the Nothing Phone 3 handles multitasking and moderate gaming smoothly. Storage options include 256GB and 512GB UFS 3.1. Connectivity includes 5G, Wi-Fi 6E, and Bluetooth 5.3, ensuring a fast and reliable performance across networks.

Camera

The triple rear camera setup includes three 50MP sensors (wide, ultrawide, and periscope telephoto), with a 50MP front-facing camera. Video recording supports 4K at 60fps. The camera delivers solid results, particularly for social media and everyday photography, though it may not surpass the top-tier flagship cameras.

Battery and Charging

The 5150 mAh battery supports 65W fast wired charging and 15W wireless charging, along with reverse wireless charging. Battery life is sufficient for a full day of moderate use, and charging is fast enough for quick top-ups during the day.

Software and Features

Nothing OS 3.5, based on Android 15, powers the Phone 3. It offers smooth gesture navigation, advanced multitasking, and long-term software updates. Integration with LED Glyph notifications and rear display enhances usability and makes the device feel unique compared to other Android phones.

Pros & Cons

    Pros:

    Unique design, LED Glyph and rear display, AMOLED display, smooth performance, solid battery.

    Cons:

    Fixed 120Hz refresh rate, price may be high relative to competitors, camera performance good but not best-in-class.

Verdict

The Nothing Phone 3 is a standout device for users who value style, innovation, and a unique smartphone experience. Its LED Glyph system, rear display, and transparent design make it highly distinctive. While some limitations exist in refresh rate and camera performance, the overall package offers excellent value for tech enthusiasts and creative users looking for a phone that turns heads.
